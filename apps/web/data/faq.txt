PRODUCT NAME: Convs AI Support Widget

1. What is Convs?
Convs is an AI-powered customer support widget designed to automatically answer customer questions, triage messages, and escalate to a human agent when needed.

2. How does Convs work?
Convs connects your website with an AI agent trained using your knowledge base. When a user asks a question, the system retrieves the most relevant information and generates a helpful response.

3. Does Convs support multiple organizations or teams?
Yes. Convs is multi-tenant. Each organization can have its own knowledge base, settings, and agents.

4. Does Convs support real-time messaging?
Yes, the widget includes real-time messaging between customers, AI, and humans.

5. Can I integrate Convs with Clerk authentication?
Yes. Convs supports Clerk for managing user and organization authentication.

6. Can the bot escalate to a human if it cannot answer?
Yes. Convs includes automated escalation logic. If confidence is low or the user requests a human, the conversation status changes to “escalated”.

7. What are conversation statuses and what do they mean?
- Unresolved: The conversation is active and waiting for an answer.
- Escalated: The bot forwarded the conversation to a human.
- Resolved: The issue is completed and closed.

8. Does Convs store customer chat history?
Yes. All messages are saved for future reference and context continuity.

9. Can I customize the bot responses or personality?
Yes. The tone, language, and rules can be adjusted in settings.

10. Does Convs require internet to work?
Yes. Since it uses an online AI model and server infrastructure, an internet connection is required.

11. What languages does Convs support?
The system supports multilingual responses depending on the model capabilities. English and Indonesian are fully supported.

12. Can I upload documents for training?
Yes. You can upload FAQs, manuals, product guides, or markdown files as knowledge sources for retrieval.
